Fever is an increase in body temperature commonly caused by infections, dehydration, or inflammation.

General care for fever includes:
- Taking adequate rest
- Drinking plenty of fluids
- Monitoring body temperature regularly

Medical attention is recommended if:
- Fever lasts more than 2 days
- Temperature exceeds 102°F (39°C)
- Fever is accompanied by severe symptoms

This information is provided for general health awareness only and is not a medical diagnosis.
