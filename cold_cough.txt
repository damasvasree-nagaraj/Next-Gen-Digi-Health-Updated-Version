Cold and cough are common respiratory conditions usually caused by viral infections or environmental changes.

General care includes:
- Adequate rest
- Warm fluids such as soups or herbal teas
- Steam inhalation to relieve congestion

Seek medical attention if:
- Symptoms persist beyond 5 to 7 days
- Breathing difficulty occurs
- High fever accompanies the cough

This content is for general guidance only and should not replace professional medical advice.
